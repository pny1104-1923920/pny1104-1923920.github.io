About Me

I was born in Federal Way, south of Seattle. Way back in the past, Federal Way was seen as an upcoming city, rivaling that of Tacoma and nearby cities, as one of the fastest growing cities in the state. But the increased crime rates has deterred Federal Way's image and has caused many negative outlooks on it's behalf.
